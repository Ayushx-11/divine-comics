DIVINE COMICS·ABOUT

A NEW UNIVERSE,
BUILT BY HAND.

Divine Comics brings you original Indian-history-inspired stories. We create digital and print comics that entertain, inspire, and connect with readers across the world.

01

THE MISSION

UNTOLD LEGENDS, REDRAWN.

Indian history is full of stories the world has never seen on a comic page — spies, generals, conspirators, twin-blade warriors, war-born giants. We bring them to life with the obsession of a manga panel and the weight of cinema.

Every page is hand-drawn. Every line is intentional. No filters. No shortcuts. Just ink, paper, and a story worth telling.

"Stories forged in history, strategy, and sacrifice."

02

THE CRAFT

DRAWN LIKE FILM.

We don't think in pages. We think in shots. Each panel is staged — scale, depth, rhythm — so the eye moves the way a camera would. The result is comics that read like cinema.

We obsess over the small things: a single fold in a turban, the weight of a khanjar, the way a shadow falls across a stone wall. The history is real. The drawing is original.

WHAT WE STAND FOR

THREE RULES.
NEVER BROKEN.

01

ORIGINAL

No re-skins. No re-tellings. Every character, every arc, written from scratch — rooted in real history but never copied from it.

02

03
