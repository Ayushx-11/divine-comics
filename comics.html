SYNOPSIS · ISSUE #1

THE AWAKENING
OF THE ENEMY.

On the edge of a kingdom that should not exist, an empire prepares to swallow Swarajya whole. The Adilshahi court is moving — generals, viziers, and the war-born Pathan army — each with their own knives drawn.

But Swarajya has its own answer. A shadow named Bahirji Naik moves through enemy walls like a whisper. Every secret he carries is a heartbeat. Every step he takes is a fuse already lit.

Shatrubodh — "knowledge of the enemy" — is an espionage epic about intelligence holding the weight of an empire, and the fearless messengers who race to carry destiny across the battlefield.
